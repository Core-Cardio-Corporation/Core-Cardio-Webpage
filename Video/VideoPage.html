Our Creation

CPR is a vital, life-saving skill that significantly increases the chances of surviving cardiac arrest. We created this video to highlight the importance of CPR and demonstrate how to perform it effectively. While our primary audience is college students, the message is universal—anyone can learn and benefit from this knowledge.

As part of our internship with the JReid Indeed Foundation through ProX, our team was tasked with educating a specific demographic about CPR. In collaboration with the incredible Justin Reid, we produced this video to empower viewers with the confidence and information needed to act in an emergency.

The video is set in a college dorm room, where two friends return from a party and one suddenly collapses. Through this scenario, we walk viewers through the essential steps of CPR and encourage them to seek professional training to deepen their understanding.
