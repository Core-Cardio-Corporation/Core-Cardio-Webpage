The JReid Indeed foundation is about Technology, Nutrition, and Athletics. The Jreid foundation also helps in the community which is why they had us come for this internship to help spread information from the American Heart association.JReid InDeed is a charitable organization founded by Justin Reid that focuses on supporting unsupported youth and communities through helpful programs, community activity, and technological access. The foundation focuses on helping children in less fortunate communities.
Justin Reid is a professional football player who plays for the Houston Texans in the NFL. He is a safety and was drafted in the third round of the 2018 NFL Draft. Reid played college football at Stanford University, where he was a standout player and earned several accolades for his performance on the field. In addition to his athletic achievements, Reid is also known for his philanthropic efforts, including his work with the JReid Indeed foundation.
